RULES AND REGULATION

Renters must be at least 21 years old, possess a valid driver’s license, and have a clean driving record. Renters under 25 may incur an additional fee.
Reservations must be made online through our official website or mobile app. Renters must provide accurate personal information, including contact details and payment information.
Full payment is required at the time of booking. A refundable security deposit will be held on the renter’s credit card until the vehicle is returned and inspected.
Vehicles must be picked up and returned at the specified location and within the agreed-upon time. Late returns may result in additional charges.
